Refund Policy

Any attendee who has purchased a ticket to Summit at Sea and is unable to attend due to any reason including but not limited to a personal matter, scheduling conflict, or force majeur must inform Summit Series via email by the following dates:

1. Cancellations filed prior to January 1, 2016 shall be refunded 100% of the cost of ticket sale less registration processing fee.
2. Cancellations filed prior to March 1, 2016 shall be refunded 75% of the cost of ticket sale.
3. Cancellations filed prior to May 1, 2016 shall be refunded 50% of the cost of ticket sale.
4. Cancellations filed prior to July 1, 2016 shall be refunded 25% of the cost of ticket sale.
5. Cancellations filed on or after July 1, 2016 shall not be refunded.

Registrations are non-transferable.

To notify Summit Series of a cancellation, please email cancellations@summitseries.com.
